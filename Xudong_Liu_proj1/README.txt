main.py is used for align all the images in /source_data, and aligned images are saved in /out_path.
        offset information is automatically saved in /info. 
        By default, .tif images will be aligned with multi-scale version and .jpg images will be aligned with 
        single-scale version

bells_whistles.py is used for align images with edge information, and aligned images are saved in /out_path/bells_whistles.
    By default, .tif images will be aligned with multi-scale version and .jpg images will be aligned with 
    single-scale version

utils.py contains the founctions and classes for all kinds of aligning.

index.html is my report.
